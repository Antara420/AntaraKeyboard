Hello! 
This is my first attemp at building functioning android app.
Decided it will be keyboard after seeing Samsung keyboard had 1gb so i decided to make my own with no trackers, spyware, keyloging or any of that.
Trying to keep it simple since my knowledge isn't best.
Alot of ideas and so little time to realize them and build the app since i can do it only in my free time.

Current features:
  -swipe up for capital letter
  -swipe left for deleting
  -swipe right for restoring deleted text
  -customising your keyboard's letter layout (letter position and long press)
  -customising your keyboard's numeric layout (what symbols we have and their longpress)
  -4 key shapes
  -customising special characters on long press
  -customising characters on numerical long press
  -programmable side buttons 
  -customising space and enter colors

Future features:
  -adding support for 3 and 4 rows
  -adding horisontal mode
  -adding emojis
  -adding all of the cyrilic support
